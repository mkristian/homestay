Welcome to "Claus Garden" with a tasty cup of coffee or tea.

You will stay in a beautiful house, with 6 large rooms and a lush spice and flowergarden.

We are an Indian/German couple (Omana and Claus), who settled here in 1984 and built this new house in 2006.

The place is still peacefull and quiet.

Many birds, butterflies, black monkeys and giant squirrels visit us.

Enjoy the fresh healthy air, pure drinking water from our own well and a perfect climate,with temperatures from 18 to 27 degree Celsius throughout the year.

You will feel at home here and sleep very well.

It's a 10 minutes walk to Kumily Busstand and Thekkady Wildlife Sanctuary.
